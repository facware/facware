2. Description of Services

Facware provides the following services to mid-market businesses:

  • IT Consulting: Operational assessment, system audits, and technology roadmapping.
  • System Architecture Design: Technical architecture scoped to your business infrastructure and growth plans.
  • Custom Software Development: Purpose-built software applications delivered against a defined outcome.
  • System & Data Integrations: Connecting existing tools (ERP, CRM, warehouse, financial systems) via APIs and custom middleware.

All paid engagements require a signed Statement of Work (SOW) or Service Agreement before any work begins. The SOW will specify:

We do not begin billable work without a fully executed agreement.

5. Payment Terms

Fixed-price model

All Facware projects are priced on a fixed-scope, fixed-price basis. The total investment is defined in the SOW. We do not bill hourly or on a time-and-materials basis unless explicitly agreed in writing.

Payment schedule

Payments are structured around project milestones as defined in the SOW. A typical schedule is:

  • Deposit (30–50%): Due upon signing the SOW, before work begins.
  • Mid-project payment(s): Due upon completion of defined milestones.
  • Final payment: Due upon final delivery and client acceptance of the deliverable.

Late payments

Invoices are due within 15 calendar days of issue unless otherwise agreed in writing. Overdue payments may result in suspension of work until the outstanding balance is settled. We reserve the right to charge a late fee of 1.5% per month on balances unpaid beyond 30 days.

Accepted payment methods

We accept bank transfers (SPEI for Mexico-based clients, wire transfer for US-based clients) and other methods as agreed in the SOW. All prices are in the currency stated in the SOW (USD or MXN).

Refund policy

Deposits are non-refundable once work has commenced. If Facware fails to deliver the defined scope as agreed, we will work to remedy the issue at no additional cost. Disputes are resolved per Section 12 (Governing Law & Dispute Resolution).
